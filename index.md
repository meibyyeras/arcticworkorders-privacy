# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ArcticWorkOrders is an internal business operations application operated by Arctic Cooling Inc.

## Account Access

ArcticWorkOrders does not support public user registration. User accounts are created and managed internally by Arctic Cooling Inc. for authorized employees and contractors only.

## Information We Collect

The application may collect and process the following information:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- Work order details
- Technician notes
- Uploaded photos
- Push notification device tokens
- Authentication identifiers
- Scheduling and dispatch information

## How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely for business operations, including:

- HVAC service management
- Scheduling and dispatch
- Customer communication
- Internal documentation
- Operational record keeping
- Push notifications related to work assignments

We do not sell personal information or use collected data for advertising purposes.

## Third-Party Services

ArcticWorkOrders uses Firebase services provided by Google, including:

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore
- Firebase Storage
- Firebase Cloud Messaging

These services may process and securely store application data.

## Data Retention

Work orders, notes, uploaded photos, and related operational records may be retained for business, legal, warranty, accounting, and customer service purposes.

Archived records may remain stored but hidden from active operational views.

## Data Security

Access to application data is restricted to authorized users and protected using authentication and Firebase security rules.
